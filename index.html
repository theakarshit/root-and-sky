Our Philosophy

A garden is not a luxury.
It is a necessity.

Root & Sky was born from a simple belief — every person deserves a green corner to breathe in. We work with families across Bengaluru and Tricity who want beautiful spaces without spending a fortune.

From plant selection to installation, we handle everything in 3 days or less. Our designers compose living spaces that grow with you.
